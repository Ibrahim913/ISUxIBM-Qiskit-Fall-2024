Overview

Welcome to ISUxIBM Qiskit Fall'24! Join us for an exhilarating journey into quantum computing. This event, organized by the Electrical and Computer Engineering Graduate Student Organization (ISU), brings together enthusiasts and experts in quantum computing for a series of workshops, tutorials, and hackathons.
