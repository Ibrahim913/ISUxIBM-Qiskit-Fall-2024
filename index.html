ISUxIBM Qiskit Fall'24

A Workshop on Quantum Computing with Hackathon/Challenges

About the Event

Welcome to ISUxIBM Qiskit Fall'24! This event, organized by the Electrical and Computer Engineering Graduate Student Organization (ISU), brings together enthusiasts and experts in quantum computing for an engaging workshop and hackathon. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced quantum developer, this event has something for everyone.

Event Details

  • Date: October 15-16, 2024
  • Location: Iowa State University, Coover Hall
  • Registration: Open until October 10, 2024

Schedule

  • Day 1: Quantum Computing Workshop - 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • Day 2: Hackathon/Challenges - 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
